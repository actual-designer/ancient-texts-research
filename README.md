## The Premise
Humanity isn't evolving; it's remembering. 

The Chronos Archive operates on a singular, cold premise: our "myths," "scriptures," and "miracles" are not metaphors, poems, or delusions. They are **corrupted technical manuals**. They are the eyewitness accounts of a high-tech precursor civilization, recorded by observers who lacked the vocabulary to describe what they were seeing.

We don't see "gods"; we see non-human operators. We don't see "heaven"; we see orbital platforms. We don't see "magic"; we see applied physics that we've simply forgotten how to calculate.

## The Methodology: Literalism & Reverse Engineering
We apply a forensic, technological lens to ancient texts to strip away the religious varnish and uncover the hardware beneath.

- **Literalism Over Metaphor:** If a text says a mountain moved, we don't look for a moral lesson—we look for acoustic levitation or tectonic manipulation.
- **The Amnesia Filter:** We treat ancient witnesses as non-technical observers describing advanced machinery. 
- **Translation Key:**
    - `Glory/Radiance` $\rightarrow$ High-energy plasma / Ionizing radiation
    - `Angels/Demons` $\rightarrow$ Bio-engineered entities / AI constructs / Suited operators
    - `Heaven` $\rightarrow$ Extra-dimensional space / Orbital enclaves
    - `Miracles` $\rightarrow$ Theoretical physics / Genetic engineering / Nanotech
