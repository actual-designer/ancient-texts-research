# Entity Registry -- Non-Human Actors Across All Sources

**Classification:** SPECIES WITH AMNESIA -- Working Hypothesis
**Scope:** All four foundational texts
**Analyst:** The Chronos Archive, Lead Investigator

---

## Organizational Hierarchy

Under the Amnesia hypothesis, the texts collectively describe a stratified non-human organization operating on and around Earth. The hierarchy has three tiers, with humans and hybrids occupying managed positions below.

```
TIER 1: COMMAND AUTHORITY (off-world / orbital)
  ├── Anu / "The Most High" / "Lord of Spirits"
  └── Council / "Assembly of the Gods"

TIER 2: SENIOR OPERATIVES (authorized ground/orbital personnel)
  ├── Enlil (Ellil) -- Ground Operations Commander
  ├── Enki (Ea) -- Chief Scientist / Engineer
  ├── Nintu (Mami/Aruru/Belet-ili) -- Bioengineering Lead
  ├── Michael, Uriel, Raphael, Gabriel -- Department Heads
  ├── Shamash (Utu) -- Solar/Surveillance Operations
  └── Ishtar (Inanna) -- Political/Reproductive Authority

TIER 3: OPERATIONAL WORKFORCE
  ├── Igigi -- Pre-human labor force (revolted)
  ├── Watchers (200) -- Rogue unit (unauthorized descent)
  ├── Cherubim / "Living Beings" -- Autonomous systems / biomechanical
  └── Various named angels -- Specialist personnel

MANAGED SPECIES
  ├── Homo sapiens -- Bioengineered labor replacement
  ├── Nephilim / Giants -- Unauthorized hybrid offspring (failed)
  ├── Gilgamesh -- Engineered hybrid ruler
  └── Enkidu -- Rapid-fabricated humanoid
```

### Anu

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Names across sources** | Anu (Atrahasis, Gilgamesh), implied as "The Most High" / "Lord of Spirits" / "Head of Days" (Enoch), "The Lord" (Ezekiel) |

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Names across sources** | "Assembly of the Gods" (Gilgamesh), "The great Anunnaki" (Atrahasis), "The Lord of Spirits and His council" (Enoch) |

| Attribute | Detail |

| **Designation** | "Evil-minded ones" (Atrahasis editorial gloss) |
| **Archive classification** | Operational-tier workforce, predecessor to human labor |

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|

## MANAGED SPECIES AND HYBRIDS

### Homo sapiens (created humans)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Created by** | Enki + Nintu/Mami (Atrahasis protocol) |
| **Substrate** | "Clay" + flesh/blood of Geshtu-E (a "god with intelligence") |
| **Purpose** | Labor replacement for the revolted Igigi |
| **Initial batch** | 14 prototypes (7M / 7F) |
| **Classification** | Managed labor species |

### Nephilim / Giants

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Source** | Enoch |
| **Produced by** | Uncontrolled interbreeding between 200 Watchers and human females |
| **Phenotype** | Extreme size, excessive metabolism, behavioral instability, aggression |
| **Outcome** | Failed hybrid organisms; incited to self-destruction by Gabriel; primary trigger for the Flood |
| **Classification** | Unauthorized hybrid offspring, genetically unstable |

### Gilgamesh

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Genome** | 2/3 non-human, 1/3 human (non-standard ratio) |
| **Designed by** | Aruru ("designed the model for his body") |
| **Parents** | Lugalbanda (father, deified king), Rimat-Ninsun (mother, goddess) |
| **Function** | King of Uruk -- engineered hybrid ruler |
| **Limitation** | Mortal despite majority non-human genome |

### Enkidu

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Created by** | Aruru (same specialist who designed Gilgamesh) |
| **Method** | Rapid fabrication from clay substrate, deployed into wilderness |
| **Purpose** | Counterpart/match for Gilgamesh |
| **Phenotype** | Extreme hairiness, initial animal-level cognition, adult-onset socialization |
| **Classification** | Rapid-fabricated humanoid, purpose-built |
