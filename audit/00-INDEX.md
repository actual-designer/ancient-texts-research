## Analytical Framework

Each entry follows this structure (per [`claude.md`](../claude.md) project directives):

1. **Source Material** -- Direct quote or reference from the text
2. **Mainstream Interpretation** -- One-sentence summary of the orthodox view
3. **The Archive's Literal Reconstruction** -- Detailed breakdown as a physical, technological occurrence
4. **Plausible Mechanism** -- Modern or theoretical scientific concept that explains the event

## Guiding Principles

- **Literalism over metaphor.** A "flying chariot that roared like thunder" is a propulsion-based vehicle.
- **The Amnesia Filter.** Witnesses lacked vocabulary for what they saw. Their accounts are technical descriptions by non-technical observers.
- **Reverse-engineer the vocabulary.** "Glory" = high-energy emission. "Angels" = suited operators or autonomous systems. "Heaven" = orbital or high-altitude platform.
- **Clinical tone.** This is forensic reconstruction, not theology.
