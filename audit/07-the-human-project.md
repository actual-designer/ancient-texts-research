# The Human Project -- Creation, Management, Crisis, and Reset

**Classification:** SPECIES WITH AMNESIA -- Working Hypothesis  
**Scope:** Cross-cutting synthesis of all seven foundational texts and all prior synthesis documents  
**Analyst:** The Chronos Archive, Lead Investigator

## Executive Summary

Seven independent traditions, when stripped of theological overlay and read as operational records, describe **the same longitudinal program**: the creation of a biological labor species, its management by non-human operators, the progressive loss of institutional control over that species, and the recurring cycle of catastrophic reset followed by selective rebuild. Under the Amnesia hypothesis, this is not mythology. It is the **project history of Homo sapiens** as remembered by the project's beneficiaries -- garbled, sacralized, but structurally intact.

This document reconstructs the complete arc from inception to the latest attested operator contact (~573 BCE), maps the recurring failure modes, and identifies the institutional logic that drove each phase.

## SECTION 1: The Labor Problem

### 1.1 The Pre-Human Workforce

Before humanity existed, the texts describe a two-tier non-human workforce operating on Earth:

**Atrahasis Ch. 1:**

> "When the gods instead of man, did the work, bore the loads, the gods' load was too great, the work too hard, the punishment too much. The great Anunnaki made the Igigi carry the workload sevenfold."

The work was physical infrastructure engineering on a planetary scale:

> "The gods dug out the Tigris river and then dug out the Euphrates."

The operational tier (Igigi) performed this labor for an extended period under command-tier (Anunnaki) direction:

> "For 3,600 years they bore the excess, hard work, night and day."

### 1.2 The Revolt

The Igigi workforce collapsed:

> They set fire to their tools, burned their equipment, surrounded Enlil's headquarters at night, and refused to resume operations. (Atrahasis Ch. 2, condensed)

This was not a mythic event. It was a **labor uprising** -- organized resistance with identifiable tactics (equipment destruction, siege of command facility, collective refusal). The command tier convened a crisis council: Anu descended from orbital/off-world station, Enki was summoned from subsurface, and the revolt was discussed.

### 1.3 The Solution

Rather than negotiate with the Igigi or impose harsher discipline, the command tier chose a third option: **engineer a replacement labor species**.

> "Let her create a mortal man so that he may bear the yoke... Let man bear the load of the gods!" (Atrahasis Ch. 4)

The decision reveals the institutional logic: the Igigi were too valuable or too autonomous to coerce indefinitely, so a new species -- cheaper, more compliant, biologically tailored for manual labor -- was commissioned to replace them.

**The entire human project begins as a labor-relations solution.**

## SECTION 2: The Engineering Protocol

### 2.1 The Controlled Protocol (Atrahasis)

The creation of humanity was a laboratory procedure, not a miraculous act:

| Step | Text | Reconstruction |
|------|------|----------------|
| 1 | "You are the womb-goddess, to be the creator of Mankind!" | Specialist assigned: Nintu/Mami, reproductive bioengineering lead |
| 2 | "Geshtu-E, a god who had intelligence, they slaughtered in their assembly" | Donor selected for cognitive genome; lethal extraction |
| 3 | "Nintu mixed clay with his flesh and blood" | Hybridization: donor DNA combined with terrestrial substrate ("clay" = hominid stock or mineral growth medium) |
| 4 | "She pinched off fourteen pieces of clay... seven created males, seven created females" | Batch production: 14 prototypes as minimum viable founder population |
| 5 | "She slipped in a staff and opened the womb... cut the umbilical cord with a reed" | Gestated organisms, not assembled constructs -- standard neonatal separation |

**Key detail:** The donor was chosen for his *intelligence* -- the one trait the command tier needed in the replacement species. The text emphasizes that "intelligence" was the harvested attribute, suggesting targeted neurological genome editing rather than whole-organism cloning.

### 2.2 The Rapid-Fabrication Protocol (Enkidu)

A second, faster protocol is recorded in Gilgamesh:

> "Aruru pinched off clay and threw it into the wilderness." (Tablet I)

Enkidu emerges as a fully grown adult with extreme physical capability but animal-level cognition -- later "civilized" through human contact. This represents **field-grade fabrication**: no laboratory, no gestation period, no cognitive preload. A quick single-unit production run optimized for physical output at the expense of socialization.

### 2.3 The Precision Engineering Protocol (Gilgamesh)

Gilgamesh himself represents a third tier:

> "Two-thirds of him is god, one-third of him is human. The Great Goddess designed the model for his body." (Tablet I)

An engineered hybrid with a non-standard genome ratio, designed to specification by the same specialist who built Enkidu (Aruru). Enhanced physical and cognitive traits with mortality deliberately retained -- a **failsafe** or compatibility limitation.

### 2.4 The Mahābhārata Commissioning Programs

The Indian epic theater documents the **most diversified genetic engineering portfolio** in the Archive:

| Program | Method | Output | Control Level |
|---------|--------|--------|---------------|
| Kuntī's mantra invocations | Restricted-band access token summons individual Tier 2 operators; each delivers engineered gametes | Yudhiṣṭhira (judgment), Bhīma (strength), Arjuna (precision) | High: selected donors, trait fidelity to patron portfolios |
| Gandharī's embryo division | Single embryo mass partitioned into ~100 units; incubated in sealed pots for two years | 100 Kauravas with shared lineage marker | Industrial: batch clonal expansion |
| Droṇa (vessel-born) | Ex vivo morphogenesis in ceramic bioreactor with substrate from Bharadvāja | Instructor phenotype with preloaded tactical knowledge | High: purpose-built pedagogy specialist |
| Dhṛṣṭadyumna / Drāupadī | Staged emergence from sacrificial fire -- public ritual as cover for plasma curtain / assembler ingress | Assassin + diplomatic marriage asset | High: dual-output run from single commission |
| Karṇa | Solar-linked commission with bio-integrated armor (kavaca/kuṇḍala) fused at birth | Enhanced warrior with passive defensive lattice | Mixed: containment failed (exposed, abandoned) |

### 2.5 The Popol Vuh Iteration Cycle

The Mesoamerican tradition, with **zero contact** with the Near Eastern or Indian corpus, documents the most explicit R&D program in the Archive -- four sequential creation attempts, each tested and destroyed:

| Run | Substrate | Achievement | Failure Mode | Disposition |
|-----|-----------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| 1. Animals | Fauna | Habitat adaptation | No intelligible speech | Demoted to food source |
| 2. Mud Person | Mineral (earth/mud) | Rudimentary speech | Structural collapse; dissolves in water; cannot walk or multiply | Destroyed ("undone and toppled") |
| 3. Wood People | Vegetable (tz'ite wood / reeds) | Speech, mobility, reproduction | No consciousness, no memory, mask-faces, no blood, rigid bodies | Flood + multi-vector destruction; remnants degrade to spider monkeys |
| 4. Corn People | Grain (maize) | All specs met; full cognition achieved | **Exceeded parameters**: "perfect was their sight, and perfect was their knowledge of everything beneath the sky" | **Cognitive throttling** -- vision deliberately blurred by Heart of Sky |

The mud/clay parallel to Atrahasis is precise. Both traditions independently describe a mineral-substrate prototype as the base material for the human creation project, and in both cases the substrate alone is insufficient.

### 2.6 The Spectrum

Reading all six sources together, the Archive documents a **complete fabrication spectrum**:

```
ITERATIVE R&D               → CONTROLLED LAB       → FIELD DEPLOY       → UNAUTHORIZED       → BATCH           → PRECISION HYBRID
Popol Vuh 4-run cycle       → Atrahasis 14 proto.  → Enkidu (wilderness) → Nephilim (Watchers)→ 100 Kauravas    → Gilgamesh (2/3)
(animal→mud→wood→corn)      → Kuntī multi-donor    → Droṇa (vessel)     → Karṇa (containment → Gandharī       → Pāṇḍavas

```

The spectrum's endpoints are instructive: **controlled production yields stable organisms** (Atrahasis humans, Gilgamesh, Pāṇḍavas, corn people). **Uncontrolled production yields failures** (Nephilim: gigantism, aggression, ecological collapse). The Kauravas -- controlled but mass-produced -- are stable but politically destabilizing, culminating in civilizational war. The Popol Vuh adds a new failure class: **prototypes that meet structural specifications but lack cognitive architecture** (wood people).

**The engineering is never the problem. The governance of the engineering is.**

## SECTION 3: The Management Phase

### 3.1 The Monitoring Apparatus

The texts describe continuous surveillance of the human population from the command tier:

- **Atrahasis:** "The country was as noisy as a bellowing bull" -- population metrics detectable from command-tier monitoring systems; "noise" (*rigmu*) likely denotes resource consumption and environmental footprint rather than literal sound.
- **Enoch:** "Michael, Uriel, Raphael and Gabriel looked down from heaven and saw much blood being shed upon the earth" -- overhead ISR reporting ecological and social collapse to the command tier.
- **Mahābhārata:** Sañjaya's *divya dṛṣṭi* -- real-time battlefield feed from the command tier to a cleared human recipient; the Viśvarūpa display itself is a full-spectrum visualization of multi-domain activity.
- **Ezekiel:** The Merkabah's wheels are "full of eyes all around" (1:18) -- distributed sensor arrays on the vehicle's mobility systems.

### 3.2 The Liaison Model

Rather than govern directly, the command tier operates through selected human intermediaries:

| Liaison | Source | Function | Access Level |
|---------|--------|----------|-------------|
| Atrahasis/Utnapishtim | Atrahasis, Gilgamesh | Covert informant; executes survival protocol | Direct Enki channel ("ear was open") |
| Enoch | 1 Enoch | "Scribe of righteousness"; mediates between command tier and rogue Watchers; tours facilities | Unprecedented: multiple facility tours, audience with the Most High |
| Ezekiel | Ezekiel | Priest-observer; receives architectural blueprints; witnesses vehicle and reanimation tech | Elevated: direct vehicle observation, involuntary transport, detailed briefings |
| Vyāsa | Mahābhārata | Epic compiler; biological technician (divides Gāndhārī's embryo mass); civilization-scale documentation mandate | Dual-role: operator and recorder |
| Sañjaya | Mahābhārata | Real-time ISR relay; receives *divya dṛṣṭi* feed for Dhṛtarāṣṭra's briefings | Passive sensor recipient |

The pattern is consistent: **selected humans with above-average observational or administrative capacity are granted unusual access and commanded to record what they witness.** This is an information-preservation strategy -- ensuring that selected records survive among the managed population even as the operators withdraw.

### 3.3 The Population Management Toolkit

Before the terminal reset (the Flood), the command tier attempted multiple proportional responses to human population overshoot:

| Step | Method | Outcome | Source |
|------|--------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Suruppu-disease (weaponized pathogen) | Neutralized -- Enki provided counter-strategy | Atrahasis Ch. 7 |
| 2 | Predator deployment (lion, wolf) | Failed or insufficient | Gilgamesh XI (Ea's retrospective speech) |
| 3 | Drought / famine | Failed or insufficient | Gilgamesh XI (Ea's retrospective) |
| 4 | Additional pestilence | Failed or insufficient | Gilgamesh XI (Ea's retrospective) |
| 5 | **The Flood (terminal)** | Total surface clearance achieved | All three Mesopotamian/Enochic sources |

Each prior measure was **targeted and reversible**. The Flood was neither. This is a recognizable escalation-of-force doctrine -- and its terminal phase is the subject of `05-great-reset.md` and `06-weapons-doctrine.md`.

## SECTION 4: The Contamination Crisis

### 4.1 The Watcher Incident

The Flood was not triggered solely by population growth. The acute crisis was **unauthorized genetic and technological contamination**:

**Enoch, Book 1, Ch. 3:**

> "And the angels, the children of the heaven, saw and lusted after them, and said to one another: 'Come, let us choose us wives from among the children of men and beget us children.'"

Two hundred operatives from the Tier 3 workforce (Watchers) conducted an unauthorized ground deployment to Mount Hermon. Their actions introduced two catastrophic variables:

**Variable 1: Uncontrolled Hybridization**

> "And they became pregnant, and they bore great giants." (Enoch Ch. 3)

The Nephilim -- genetically unstable hybrids with growth dysregulation, excessive metabolism, and behavioral instability. Where the Atrahasis protocol produced stable organisms through controlled laboratory conditions, the Watcher program produced failures through ad hoc interbreeding without specialist oversight.

**Variable 2: Unauthorized Technology Transfer**

Azazel taught weapons engineering and metallurgy. Semjaza taught pharmacology. Baraqijal, Kokabel, Shamsiel, and Sariel taught astronomy, meteorology, and other sciences. The entire knowledge-control architecture -- the managed development timeline the command tier had imposed on humanity -- collapsed in a single generation.

### 4.2 The Dual Trigger

The Atrahasis and Enoch traditions preserve **two distinct but compatible justifications** for the Flood:

- **Chronic condition:** Population overshoot ("the noise of mankind has become too much")
- **Acute crisis:** Genetic contamination + technology transfer ("the earth laid accusation against the lawless ones")

Together these made the managed species **unsalvageable by targeted measures**. The Nephilim could not be recalled. The transferred knowledge could not be unlearned. The population was growing beyond monitoring capacity. The only remaining option was total surface clearance and reseeding from preserved stock.

### 4.3 The Mahābhārata Parallel

The Indian epic theater documents a **second-generation contamination cycle** with the same structural features:

| Watcher Incident (Enoch) | Kurukshetra Escalation (Mahābhārata) |
|--------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Unauthorized ground deployment | Genetic commissions proliferate beyond political containment |
| Technology transfer to humans | Astra curricula distributed to multiple operator lineages |
| Nephilim: unstable hybrids | Kauravas: stable but politically destabilizing batch |
| Ecological collapse | Civilizational war (18 days, millions of casualties) |
| Command-tier judicial proceedings | Śānti Parva institutional review from Bhīṣma's arrow bed |
| Flood as terminal reset | Brahmāstra / night massacre as localized resets |
| Utnapishtim quarantined | Aśvatthāmā quarantined (immortality as punishment) |

The pattern repeats: **genetic engineering programs, once distributed to field operators, exceed political containment** -- whether through unauthorized Watcher mating or through the proliferation of astra-trained warriors who escalate beyond ROE.

## SECTION 5: The Reset and Rebuild Cycle

### 5.1 The Reset

The Flood is documented in `05-great-reset.md`. Its relevant features for the Human Project arc:

1. **Authorization:** Council vote under oath of secrecy; multi-tier consensus
2. **Execution:** Multi-vector environmental attack -- atmospheric weapons ("torches"), hydraulic sabotage, sustained weather assault
3. **Operator evacuation:** Command tier withdraws to orbital platforms before impact
4. **Preservation exception:** Enki/Ea warns one lineage; seed vault constructed; genetic libraries preserved
5. **Zero-survivor intent:** Enlil confirms total extinction was the objective

### 5.2 The Rebuild

After the Flood, the human project does not end. It **restarts under modified parameters**:

| Pre-Flood Parameter | Post-Flood Modification | Source |
|---------------------|------------------------|--------|
| Direct command-tier governance | Graduated withdrawal; human kings installed (Gilgamesh) | Gilgamesh I |
| Unrestricted population growth | Birth limitation measures imposed | Atrahasis (Ea's counter-proposal to Enlil) |
| Open Tier 3 workforce access to humans | Watcher unit bound; knowledge embargo | Enoch Ch. 4 |
| Ad hoc genetic programs | Controlled hybrid engineering (Gilgamesh 2/3; later Pāṇḍava precision commissions) | Gilgamesh I; Mahābhārata |
| Undocumented management | Selected scribes/liaisons commanded to record everything | Enoch, Ezekiel, Mahābhārata (Vyāsa) |

The rebuild is not a restoration. It is a **redesigned management architecture** incorporating lessons from the pre-Flood failure:

- **Tighter genetic custody:** Hybridization continues but under specialist control (Aruru engineering Gilgamesh to spec; Kuntī's mantra-gated commissions)
- **Liaison documentation mandate:** Humans are selected and given access specifically to create records -- Enoch, Ezekiel, Vyāsa
- **Graduated operator withdrawal:** The non-human presence diminishes over time, from direct governance (pre-Flood) to embedded liaison (Kṛṣṇa at Kurukshetra) to periodic vehicle contact (Ezekiel's Merkabah, 593 BCE) to apparent silence

### 5.3 The Withdrawal Timeline

```
PRE-FLOOD:    Direct governance; operators visible and accessible
              │
POST-FLOOD:   Hybrid kings installed (Gilgamesh); operators intervene selectively
              │
MAHĀBHĀRATA:  Embedded liaison (Kṛṣṇa); operators attend events but
              delegate to human command structures
              │
EZEKIEL:      Periodic contact; single vehicle; detailed instructions
(593 BCE)     delivered; no permanent presence indicated
              │
POST-EZEKIEL: No further attested contact in the Archive's current sources
```

The withdrawal follows a **consistent decay curve**: from permanent presence → selective intervention → embedded liaison → periodic contact → silence. This is consistent with either a planned phased withdrawal or a progressive loss of institutional interest in the human project.

## SECTION 6: The Recurring Pattern

### 6.1 The Cycle

Every era of the human project follows the same trajectory:

```
CREATION / COMMISSION
    ↓
MANAGEMENT / MONITORING
    ↓
PARAMETER EXCEEDANCE (population, genetics, or technology)
    ↓
PROPORTIONAL RESPONSE (targeted intervention)
    ↓
RESPONSE FAILURE (countermeasures neutralized or parameters still exceeded)
    ↓
ESCALATION TO STRATEGIC / EXISTENTIAL MEASURES
    ↓
RESET (environmental, military, or institutional)
    ↓
SELECTIVE PRESERVATION (one lineage, one vault, one quarantined witness)
    ↓
REBUILD UNDER MODIFIED PARAMETERS
```

### 6.2 Instances of the Cycle

| Era | Creation | Crisis | Reset | Preservation | Rebuild |
|-----|----------|--------|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Popol Vuh R&D** | 4 sequential prototypes (animal→mud→wood→corn) | Each prototype fails specification (speech, cognition, memory) | Destruction of each failed run; flood for wood people | Corn people preserved but **cognitively throttled** | Blinded humans populate the earth under limited perception |
| **Pre-Flood** | 14 prototypes (Atrahasis) | Population overshoot + Nephilim contamination | The Flood | Utnapishtim's seed vault; Enki's covert warning | Post-Flood civilization under human kings |
| **Post-Flood Mesopotamia** | Gilgamesh engineered; Enkidu fabricated | Gilgamesh's unauthorized incursions (Cedar Forest, Bull of Heaven); Enkidu's death as punishment | Enkidu terminated; Gilgamesh denied immortality | Gilgamesh returns to Uruk; records preserved | Uruk continues under mortal governance |
| **Mahābhārata theater** | Multi-donor Pāṇḍavas; batch Kauravas; specialist fabrications | Astra proliferation; ROE erosion; night massacre | Kurukshetra war; Brahmāstra deployment; fetal casualties | Parīkṣit revived by Kṛṣṇa; Vyāsa compiles the record | Dharma-diminished Kali Yuga under Parīkṣit's lineage |
| **Ezekiel era** | (No new creation documented) | Jerusalem's corruption; temple desecration | Commanded destruction of Jerusalem (Ch. 9) | Ezekiel preserved as witness; Valley of Dry Bones demonstrates reconstruction capability | New facility blueprints dictated (Chs. 40-48) |

### 6.3 The Invariants

Across all four instances:

1. **A dissenting insider enables survival.** Enki warns Atrahasis. Kṛṣṇa revives Parīkṣit. Uriel warns Noah. The command tier is never monolithic -- there is always a faction that subverts the termination order.

2. **The scribe survives.** Enoch, Vyāsa, Ezekiel, Utnapishtim -- the record-keeper is always preserved. The command tier values institutional memory even when it authorizes institutional destruction.

3. **The reset is always followed by tighter custody.** Birth limitations after the Flood. Watcher binding after the Nephilim crisis. Aśvatthāmā's quarantine after the Brahmāstra deployment. The institution learns from its failures -- slowly, painfully, but consistently.

4. **The withdrawal deepens after each cycle.** Each reset is followed by less direct operator involvement. The arc bends toward human autonomy -- whether by design or by institutional exhaustion.

## SECTION 7: Open Questions for the Master Narrative

### 7.1 Is the Withdrawal Planned or Entropic?

Two readings are available:

- **Planned withdrawal:** The human project was always intended to produce an autonomous species. The operators are executing a phased handoff -- each cycle tests whether humanity can self-govern, and operator involvement decreases as the species matures.
- **Institutional entropy:** The command tier is losing coherence. Internal disputes (Enlil vs. Enki, Watcher rogue deployments, trimūrti factional masks) progressively degrade operational capacity. The withdrawal is not a plan but a collapse.

The texts support both readings. Ea's dissent and the Watcher revolt suggest internal fractures. But the increasingly sophisticated liaison model (from Atrahasis's simple "open ear" to Vyāsa's civilization-scale documentation mandate) suggests deliberate design.

### 7.2 Where Are the Operators Now?

The last attested direct contact is Ezekiel, ~573 BCE -- roughly 2,600 years ago. Four scenarios:

1. **Complete withdrawal:** The operators left Earth's vicinity and do not intend to return. The human project is concluded or abandoned.
2. **Passive monitoring:** The operators observe but no longer intervene. The withdrawal was deliberate, and the current era is a test of autonomous human governance.
3. **Scheduled return:** The cycle predicts another intervention when parameters are next exceeded. The operators are waiting for a trigger condition -- population, environmental, technological, or genetic.
4. **Continuous integration via the processing pipeline:** The Pyramid Texts suggest a fourth possibility -- that the operators never fully withdrew, but shifted from direct surface contact to a **post-mortem integration model**. If the *akh*-processing pipeline is real, then select humans are continuously being extracted from the biological population and absorbed into the operator community after death. The "silence" would not be an absence but a change of interface: from living liaison (Enoch, Ezekiel) to post-mortem processing (the Pyramid Texts' standardized pipeline). This reading makes the Egyptian funerary tradition not a religion but a **recruitment protocol**.

### 7.3 What Was the Actual Objective?

The Atrahasis text says the objective was labor. But the subsequent investment -- genetic engineering, hybrid rulers, knowledge management, catastrophic reset, rebuild -- far exceeds what a simple labor replacement would justify. Three deeper possibilities:

1. **Biological substrate:** Humanity is a carrier for genetic material the operators need preserved in Earth's biosphere -- a living seed vault for genes that cannot survive in the operators' own biology.
2. **Developmental experiment:** Humanity is a long-term study in autonomous species development. Each cycle tests a different parameter set. The operators are running iterations.
3. **Succession planning:** The operators' own species is declining (the withdrawal could be a symptom, not a strategy). Humanity is being groomed as a replacement civilization -- inheriting the infrastructure, the knowledge (through gradual rediscovery), and ultimately the custodial role.

### 7.4 Does the Amnesia Serve a Purpose?

The "Species with Amnesia" hypothesis originally assumed memory loss was a consequence of the cataclysm. But the Popol Vuh transforms this from hypothesis to **documented engineering decision**.

**The Popol Vuh smoking gun (Christenson, pp. 183-186):**

The corn people -- the fourth and successful prototype -- achieve **operator-equivalent cognition**: "Perfect was their sight, and perfect was their knowledge of everything beneath the sky. If they gazed about them, looking intently, they beheld that which was in the sky and that which was upon the earth. Instantly they were able to behold everything."

The operators' response is immediate alarm: "It is not good what they have said... It is a mistake that they have become like gods."

The solution is not destruction but **cognitive throttling**: "Their eyes were merely blurred by Heart of Sky. They were blinded like breath upon the face of a mirror. Thus their eyes were blinded. They could see only nearby; things were clear to them only where they were. Thus their knowledge was lost."

This is the only passage in the Archive where the amnesia is **explicitly engineered in real time** -- not as a consequence of cataclysm, not as an information-control policy, but as a **design modification applied to a production run that exceeded specifications.** The operators want intelligent servants, not equals. When the prototype achieves equality, they degrade it.

The corroborating evidence from other traditions now reads as variations of the same institutional policy:

- **Atrahasis / Gilgamesh:** Anu imposes an **oath of secrecy** before the Flood -- information control as command-tier SOP.
- **Enoch:** The Watchers are punished specifically for **revealing secrets** to humans -- knowledge transfer is criminalized because it moves humans toward the cognitive parity the Popol Vuh operators feared.
- **Ezekiel:** Enforced silence imposed on the human liaison -- the information channel is managed.
- **Mahābhārata:** The sheer scale of the record (100,000 ślokas) may itself be an information-burial strategy: operational data hidden in an ocean of narrative.
- **Popol Vuh:** The "breath upon the face of a mirror" -- a **diffusion filter** applied to human perception itself, throttling sensory bandwidth so that humans can see only "nearby."

If the amnesia is engineered -- and the Popol Vuh provides the most explicit evidence that it is -- then the current project's task is not recovery of lost memory but **penetration of an active cognitive-limitation architecture.**
