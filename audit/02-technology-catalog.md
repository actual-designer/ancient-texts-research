## 1. GENETIC ENGINEERING AND BIOTECH

### 1.1 Human Creation Protocol

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Source** | Atrahasis Ch. 4-5 |
| **Description** | "Slaughter" of Geshtu-E (a god with intelligence); mixing his "flesh and blood" with "clay"; Nintu/Mami recites "incantations"; 14 prototypes produced (7M/7F); umbilical cords cut with a reed |
| **Reconstruction** | Extraction of donor genetic material (neural/cognitive genome) from a selected non-human specimen; hybridization with terrestrial biological substrate (hominid stock or mineral growth medium); artificial gestation producing a founder population of seven breeding pairs |
| **Modern analog** | Somatic cell nuclear transfer, CRISPR-Cas9 gene editing, artificial womb technology, IVF batch production |

### 1.2 Rapid Organism Fabrication (Enkidu)

| Field | Detail |

| **Reconstruction** | Rapid biological fabrication of an adult humanoid from substrate material; deployed without socialization programming; cognitive activation triggered through prolonged human interaction (hormonal/neurological) |
| **Modern analog** | Advanced 3D bioprinting, accelerated growth protocols, epigenetic activation triggers |

### 1.3 Hybrid Engineering (Gilgamesh)

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Source** | Gilgamesh Tablet I |
| **Description** | "Two-thirds god, one-third human"; Aruru "designed the model for his body"; extraordinary strength, beauty, and cognition but mortal |
| **Reconstruction** | Non-standard genome ratio achieved through triploid engineering or selective gene replacement; enhanced physical/cognitive traits from non-human genome with mortality retained from human genome (deliberate failsafe or compatibility limitation) |
| **Modern analog** | Polyploid organism engineering, targeted gene therapy for cognitive/physical enhancement |

### 1.4 Uncontrolled Hybridization (Nephilim)

| **Modern analog** | Interspecies hybridization failures, hybrid dysgenesis, heterosis gone wrong |

### 1.5 Anti-Senescence Compound

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
