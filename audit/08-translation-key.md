## How to Use This Document

This glossary translates pre-scientific vocabulary used by ancient witnesses into their most plausible technical referents under the Amnesia hypothesis. Terms are grouped by operational domain. Each entry lists the archaic term(s), the source(s) where it appears, and the Archive's technical interpretation.

Use this document as a cross-reference when reading any analysis in the Archive: whenever an archaic term appears, look up its technical meaning here to maintain a consistent decoding framework.
