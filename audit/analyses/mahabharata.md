## Executive Summary

The Mahābhārata is the archive's densest operational dossier: a longitudinal record of command-tier interventions, genetic commissioning protocols, directed-energy and mass-effect weapons, aerospace logistics, and large-scale combined-arms warfare framed in devotional vocabulary. Under the Amnesia hypothesis it reads less as theology than as **after-action folklore** -- survivors and redactors describing hardware and biology they could not name. Key reconstructions:

1. **Vimanas** as vectored-thrust or field-propulsion aerospace platforms with documented interiors, carrying capacity, and radiant hull phenomena comparable to the Merkabah/crystal-throne class vehicles ([ezekiel-merkabah.md](ezekiel-merkabah.md), [enoch-watchers.md](enoch-watchers.md)).
2. **Astras** as tier-restricted weapons packages (kinetic, thermal, atmospheric, and possibly radiological/neutron-flux analogues), released through biometric/speech-linked command authorization -- functionally similar to "calling down" orbital or platform-based strikes and to Shamash's **Thirteen Winds** in Gilgamesh ([gilgamesh.md](gilgamesh.md)).
3. **Birth commissions** (Kuntī's mantra-invocations; births from fire or receptacles) as **in vitro gestation, surrogate substrate, or direct phenotype insertion** by senior operatives -- parallel to Nintu/Aruru clay protocols and Watcher hybridization outcomes ([atrahasis.md](atrahasis.md), [enoch-watchers.md](enoch-watchers.md)).
4. **Kurukshetra** as a **combined-arms theater**: trench logistics, formation doctrine (`vyūhas`), integrated aerial observation (chariot-mounted liaison officers who are non-human or augmented), and intermittent escalation to strategic-grade weapons.
5. **The Viśvarūpa ("universal form")** as either **full-spectrum sensor/visual warfare display** (multi-band emission mapped onto a humanoid volumetric projection) or induced perceptual override -- comparable to exposure to high-information-density tactical HUDs or radiation-induced visionary states.
6. **Hierarchy mapping**: Brahmā–Viṣṇu–Śiva cluster as **Tier 1 / supreme coordination personas** (possibly factional or departmental rather than three discrete beings); Indra–Agni–Vāyu–Varuṇa–Yama as **Tier 2 theater commands**; Gandharva–Apsaras–Rākṣasa–Nāga as **Tier 3 workforce, client species, or uplifted/chimeric lines** ([01-entity-registry.md](../01-entity-registry.md)).
7. **Immortality motifs** (amṛta, ciranjīvin status, Aśvatthāmā's curse) as **life-extension biochemistry, indefinite suspended aging, and permanent operational quarantine** -- Gilgamesh's plant-of-youth parallel ([gilgamesh.md](gilgamesh.md)).
